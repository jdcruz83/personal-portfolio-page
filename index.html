Information about me

I am Jorge, I am graduated in Marketing (Comunicação Organizacional at Escola Superior de Educação de Coimbra), and I am studying web development. In the past couple of years, during the pandemic, when layoffs sent us home, I felt the need to study and improve myself. Programming was always a hobby for me, and I wanted to give it a try.

As I was progressing in the process of obtaining certificates, first in Phyton, then on HTML and CSS, I started to discover that this was not a hobby but a passion. And that passion drove me here, to this portfolio, and I hope it can drive me through a great career in this area. I am extremely dedicated to make a career change and become a web developer.
